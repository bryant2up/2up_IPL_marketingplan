1. Campaign Strategy & Positioning
⚠️ CRITICAL: Surrogate Marketing Required
Position as "Cricket Entertainment Platform" NOT "Betting Site". Direct gambling ads are banned in India. Use: Predict, Test, Challenge, Join. NEVER use: Bet, Gamble, Wager, Casino.
Core Messaging
Frame as:
• Test your cricket knowledge
• Predict match outcomes
• Join cricket community
• Experience IPL excitement

Why This Works:
• Avoids gambling stigma
• Positions as skill-based
• Taps into cricket pride
• Legal compliance for Facebook ads
Language Split
50% Hindi Ads:
• Target 40% of India population
• Tier 2-3 cities
• Example: "आज का मैच कौन जीतेगा?"

50% English Ads:
• Urban, educated demographic
• Higher income
• Example: "Predict Today's IPL Winner"

Test both, scale winner after 2 weeks

Account Warming Protocol (Critical):

Week 3+ (Mar 22+): Full campaign launch
Account Purpose Launch Date Daily Budget (Peak)
IN_ACC_01 Primary - Hindi Creatives Mar 10 $75-100
IN_ACC_02 Primary - English Creatives Mar 10 $75-100
IN_ACC_03 Backup - Ready to activate If needed $75-100
IN_ACC_04 Backup - Ready to activate If needed $75-100
Business Manager Setup
  • Create 2 separate Business Managers
  • Each BM has 2 ad accounts
  • Different payment methods per BM
  • Different email addresses
  • Store all creatives in cloud backup
Backup Strategy
  • 2-3 mirror domains registered (e.g., 2up-in.com)
  • All creatives in Google Drive
  • Can redeploy in 24 hours if account banned
  • Daily ad account health monitoring
  • Never put all budget in one account
